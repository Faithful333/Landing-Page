OverviewOur Commitment to Your Privacy

Faithful Ventures ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our website or engages with our programs. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using our website at faithfulventures.com, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name and email address when you join our waitlist or register interest in our programs
  • Information you share when applying to our Pitch Competition or Venture Builder Cohort

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This is collected via standard analytics tools.

02How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

03Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We use Supabase for data storage, which maintains enterprise-grade security standards. While we take reasonable precautions, no transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law.

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and collect usage data. These may include:

  • Essential cookies required for the website to function

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@faithfulventures.com.
